Stock Photos that don't suck

Finding stock photo sites is a challenge - let us help

Tired of going down the deep underground dungeon of the sites that promise good, free stock photos?

Everybody has searched for that elusive stock photo, especially for open source projects. After all, hours of work is being provided for the betterment of the whole development community, why not stock photos?

The problem with free stock photos is that they are either low-res amateur photos, cheesy photos of people wearing headsets, or bait-and-switch type operators where they either spam you with incessant emails, or you don't truly get value.

Nevertheless below is an ongoing list of the best stock photo sites that we, at synapticloop, have actually used. The photos on these github pages are sourced from them.

  • Unsplash http://unsplash.com/ Free (do whatever you want) high-resolution photos. They also have an option to be emailed 10 free High-Res photos every day - Nice!
  • Gratisography http://www.gratisography.com/ Free high-resolution pictures you can use on your personal and commercial projects.
  • ISO Republic http://isorepublic.com/ Free stock photos for creatives.
  • Picography http://picography.co/ No muss, free images to use however you like. Made in Ireland
  • Death to the Stock Photo http://join.deathtothestockphoto.com/ You have to sign up to their mailing list to be sent a pack of photos every month. No spam, just a quality pack of photos every month.
